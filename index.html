About Us:

 

Description:

This website is for people seeking information about AGHM. Our formula is simple and effective. We designed this website that is tailored to the interests of an individual, with maximum freedom to discover the exotic exhibits. The museum documents the history of Georgia and North Florida, spanning more than 200 years. Artifacts, photographs, documents, and a variety of interactive permanent exhibits tell the story of settlement.


Goal:

Our goal is to reach a broad audience through our outstanding collections, statewide outreach, and dynamic programming. We believe that an understanding of the past changes lives and builds better communities.


Business Objective:

We, Simply believe in presenting AGHM in a way that is absolutey unique and unexplored. Our mission is to help travelers explore the history and depth of AGHM with highest level of satisfaction.


Website Objective:

To ensure that business objectives and goals are achieved successfully. We also aim to help all the individuals across the globe choose that particular experience which will shape your vision of the AGHM by providing a user-friendly platform.

 

 

 

Services We Provide:

 

     This website provides the comprehensive information about everything that is available in AGHM. The AGHM will have both conventional exhibits, e.g., documents, drawings, and photographs, as well as scale models of some of the important Forts, e.g., Fort Frederica and the Castillo de San Marcos. Allows the user to register themselves and become members. Members enjoy free access to permanent and special traveling exhibitions. This website also helps an individual to reach the musuem with the help of Visit Us option that is available. AGHM offers widest variety of special event options to be hosted at one location. Hosting your event at AGHM not only provides a stunning and unique backdrop for your event but also supports our mission to connect people, history, and culture through exhibitions, educational history programs and the preservation of documents and photographs. The gallery helps one to have a look at the exotic exhibits.
